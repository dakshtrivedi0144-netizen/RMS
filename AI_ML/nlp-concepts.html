Identifying entities like names, dates, and locations in text. Crucial for information extraction and knowledge base building.
Extraction

Determining emotional tone and opinions in text. Used for customer feedback analysis, social media monitoring, and brand perception.

Language Models
Predicting next words in sequence. Foundation for text generation, machine translation, and conversational AI systems.
Advanced

Converting words to numerical vectors (Word2Vec, GloVe, FastText). Captures semantic relationships and enables deep learning models.

RNNs, LSTMs, and GRUs process sequential data. Ideal for tasks requiring context from previous inputs like machine translation.
Deep Learning
Transformers
Attention-based architectures revolutionizing NLP. Enable parallel processing and capture long-range dependencies efficiently.

Generation
