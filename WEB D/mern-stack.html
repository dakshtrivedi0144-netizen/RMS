Engineering Roadmap

Basic: Core Integration

Connecting the pieces into a working system.

  • Mongoose Models & Validations
  • Express Middlewares & Routing
  • React State Management (Hooks)

Mid: Professional Grade

Security, complex states, and performance.

  • JWT & Cookie Authentication

Real-time systems and professional ops.
