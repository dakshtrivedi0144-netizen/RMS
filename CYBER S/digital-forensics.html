🛡️ Introduction
Digital Forensics in IoT Security focuses on investigating cyber incidents involving Internet of Things devices, networks, and data. It involves collecting, preserving, analyzing, and presenting digital evidence to understand security breaches, data breaches, and malicious activities in IoT ecosystems, ensuring compliance and improving security postures.
