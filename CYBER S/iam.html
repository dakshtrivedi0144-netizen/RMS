Introduction

Identity and Access Management (IAM) is a framework of policies and technologies for ensuring that the right users have the appropriate access to technology resources. It encompasses authentication, authorization, and user management to protect sensitive data and systems from unauthorized access.

Learning Roadmap

Beginner Level
  • Understand basic concepts: Identity, Authentication, Authorization
  • Learn about user directories (LDAP, Active Directory)
  • Study authentication methods (passwords, MFA, biometrics)
  • Explore access control models (RBAC, ABAC)
Intermediate Level
  • Dive into federation protocols (SAML, OAuth, OpenID Connect)
  • Learn cloud IAM (AWS IAM, Azure AD, GCP IAM)
  • Understand compliance and governance (GDPR, HIPAA)
  • Study IAM tools and solutions (Okta, Ping Identity)
Advanced Level
  • Implement zero-trust architecture
  • Master identity governance and administration (IGA)
  • Learn about threat detection and response in IAM
  • Explore emerging technologies (blockchain for identity, AI in access control)
